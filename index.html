// FAQ

Questions

Is the panel compatible with all versions of After Effects?
Neyko FX is compatible with After Effects CC 2021 and later (version 18+), on both Windows and macOS.
Does the panel require an internet connection?
Yes, an internet connection is required to communicate with the AI. The panel itself is installed locally inside After Effects.
Can we add or remove users at any time?
Yes. You can add seats or remove users at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is adjusted accordingly.
What languages does the AI speak?
The panel is fully bilingual — French and English. Users can switch between languages with a single click.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No long-term commitment. Plans are monthly and can be cancelled at any time. We offer a free demo before any purchase.
